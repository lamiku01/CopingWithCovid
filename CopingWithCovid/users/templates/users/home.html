Hello, and Welcome!

This app is a project initiated by the Kent School of Social Work at the University of Louisville.it was created by groups of Computer Science and Engineering students from Speed School of Engineering. 

Pandemics can lead to increases in anxiety, depression, trauma, and obsessive-compulsive and other mental health symptoms.Quarantine can have significant negative impacts on couple and parent-child relationships, as well as leading to feelings of social isolation/loneliness.Mental health issues have been identified as the emerging pandemic in response to COVID 19. For those reasons, this App wants to address those problems. 

Purpose:

  • To provide Students and Faculty a way to monitor and improve their Cognitive, Physical, Psychological, and Social needs
  • Educate Students and Faculty about the importance of staying healthy physically, psychologically, socially, and cognitively.

Note*: This app is under development.
