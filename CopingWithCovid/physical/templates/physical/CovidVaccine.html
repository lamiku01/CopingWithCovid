Covid Vaccine

CDC Vaccine Rollot Recommendations

The Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) has recommened the following phases for vaccine distribution:

  • Phase 1A:
    • Healthcare Personnel
    • Long-term Care Facility Residents
    • Recommended on December 3, 2020
  • Phase 1B:
    • Frontline essential workers
    • People aged 75 years and older
    • Recommended on December 22, 2020
  • Phase 1C:
    • People aged 65-74 years
    • People aged 16-64 years with underlying medical conditions
    • Recommended on December 22, 2020

Some U.S states have recognized the following phases and began vaccine distributions based on these phases. These phases have not been recommended by the CDC.For more information please visit cdc.gov

  • Phase 2:
    • People aged 40 years and older
  • Phase 3:
    • People aged 16 years and older

Please check with your State of residence to determine your eligibiity for the COVID-19 Vaccine.
