Exercise

Exercising at a moderate intensity, such as taking brisk walks, for 150 minutes or more a week can have tremendous benefits on your physical and mental health. Some of these benefits include:

  • Reduce risk of cardiovascular disease, type 2 diabetes, and some cancers
  • Reduce risk and manage symptoms of anxiety and depression
  • Manage weight
  • Fall asleep quicker and stay asleep longer
  • Improve sexual health
  • Strengthen bones and muscles
